Personal Profile

Electronics and communication engineer with PhD in Physics having vast experience in numerical modeling and simulations of nonlinear and stochastic phenomenon and their engineering applications. Expertise in designing nonlinear dynamics based futurisitic logic gates using logical stochastic resonance and chaos computing. Employs time series analysis techniques and data driven models to understand variable star dynamics, heart rate variability and complex networks.

Research Interests

  • Chaos Computing and Logical Stochastic Resonance
  • Noise Induced Phenomenon
  • Stellar variability and RR Lyrae stars
  • Synchronization and stability analysis of Complex Networks
  • Machine learning, Deep neural networks and Compressive sensing
  • Hands on Table top experiments with Electronics circuits and lasers
