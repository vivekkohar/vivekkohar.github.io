Taekwondo Medals in the 14th Punjab State Taekwondo Championship

November 4th, 2012

Vivek Kohar from our institute participated in 14th Punjab State Taekwondo Championships representing Mohali district and won Gold medal in Sparring and Bronze in Muye competition. Consequently, he has been selected to represent Punjab in the National Games to be held later this year.

Self Defence/Taekwondo Camp

October 30th, 2012

We are organizing a Self Defence Workshop / Taekwondo Camp for about one month starting 30th October 2012.Training sessions of about one hour duration will be held thrice a week from 7:30-8:30 PM. It will be sort of first exposure to martial arts so that interested people can have an idea and pursue further. The instructor will be Master Satpal Singh Rehal who is currently Black Belt 5th Dan and currently is the General Secretary of Punjab Taekwondo Association.
